About

I started out building small web projects with PHP and Java, which pulled me toward Computer Science and Engineering. I'm now in my third year, sharpening my fundamentals in data structures & algorithms, networking, and machine learning, while working toward building something of my own — I'm drawn to the idea of starting a company someday, likely in digital marketing or freelance web development.
